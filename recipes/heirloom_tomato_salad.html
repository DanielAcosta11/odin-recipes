Heirloom Tomato Salad with Rosemary

Description:

You can never go wrong with an heirloom tomato salad, whether it's the dog days of summer or the subzero days of winter. Perhaps you'd want a cold, refreshing salad with colorful veggies and fruits, then this exact recipe is for you! If not, lucky for you, tomatoes are a versatile fruit! Cook the tomatoes on a barbecue grill and et voila, you've got yourself an amazing side dish.

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves removed and finely chopped
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3 large heirloom tomatoes, quartered
  • 3 small heirloom tomatoes, quartered
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, rosemary, and oregano together in a large bowl. Add tomatoes and toss until evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, 10 to 15 minutes.

  2. Season with salt and black pepper. Toss again before serving.
